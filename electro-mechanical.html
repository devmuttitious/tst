Expertise in Electro Mechanical Engineering

Welcome to TST Company, your trusted partner in delivering cutting-edge electromechanical engineering solutions. With a strong reputation and expertise, we specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining essential systems that ensure the seamless operation of buildings and infrastructure projects.

Our Electromechanical Engineering Services

a. Electrical Engineering
TST Company provides comprehensive electrical engineering services, covering the entire scope of power supply and distribution, ensuring efficient and safe electrical systems for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

1. Power Distribution: We design robust power distribution systems tailored to meet the specific needs of each project, including transformers, switchgear, and load management solutions.

2. Lighting Systems: Our creates energy-efficient lighting designs for both indoor and outdoor environments, enhancing safety and visibility.

3. Backup Power Solutions: We provide reliable backup power systems, including UPS and generators, to ensure continuous operations during outages.

b. Fire Fighting Systems
We offer specialized fire protection solutions that include the design, installation, and maintenance of fire suppression systems to safeguard lives and properties.

1. Fire Sprinkler Systems: Our automated fire sprinkler systems are designed for rapid response, effectively containing fires and reducing damage.

2. Fire Alarm Systems: We install advanced fire detection and alarm systems, providing early warning to ensure quick evacuation and emergency response.

3. Fire Extinguishing Systems: TST offers specialized fire extinguishing solutions, including gas suppression and foam-based systems for high-risk areas.

c. Water Drainage Systems
Our water drainage services include the planning and installation of efficient drainage systems that effectively manage rainwater and wastewater, preventing flooding and water damage.

1. Stormwater Drainage: We design and implement stormwater drainage systems to efficiently manage runoff and prevent water accumulation during heavy rains.

2. Sewage Systems: Our handles the installation of comprehensive sewage networks, ensuring proper wastewater disposal and compliance with environmental standards.

3. Pumping Stations: We install and maintain pumping stations to facilitate the movement of wastewater and stormwater, ensuring reliable and continuous operation.
