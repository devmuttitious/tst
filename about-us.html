FAQ

What is TST?

TST is a leading civil engineering and construction company based in Saudi Arabia, specializing in infrastructure development, building construction, and project management services.

Which services does TST offer?

TST offers a wide range of services, including civil engineering, architectural design, infrastructure projects, project management, and consulting services tailored to meet client needs.

How can I start a project with TST?

To start a project, contact our via email at contact@tst.com.sa or visit our office for a detailed consultation and project proposal.

What types of projects does TST handle?

TST handles a diverse range of projects, including residential buildings, commercial spaces, industrial facilities, and public infrastructure projects.

Does TST provide project management services?

Yes, TST offers comprehensive project management services, including planning, scheduling, resource allocation, and quality control to ensure successful project completion.

How does TST ensure the quality of its projects?

TST follows stringent quality assurance protocols and employs the latest construction technologies to maintain the highest standards of quality and safety in all projects.

Can I invest in a TST project?

Yes, TST welcomes investors for various projects. Please contact our business development for more details on investment opportunities.

What is TST's approach to sustainability?

TST is committed to sustainable building practices, incorporating eco-friendly materials and innovative designs to reduce environmental impact and promote energy efficiency.
