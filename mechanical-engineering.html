Welcome to TST Company, your trusted partner in delivering cutting-edge mechanical engineering solutions. With a wealth of experience, we excel in providing innovative and efficient services, from product design to large-scale industrial systems. Our expertise spans across a diverse range of mechanical engineering projects tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

Our Mechanical Engineering Services

a. Product Design and Development
TST Company specializes in the design and development of mechanical products, utilizing state-of-the-art CAD software and advanced prototyping techniques to bring innovative solutions to market.

1. 3D Modeling and Simulation: Our engineers use the latest 3D modeling tools to create detailed and accurate product designs.

2. Prototype Development: We offer rapid prototyping services to test and refine product concepts efficiently.

3. Manufacturing Optimization: We streamline the design process to enhance manufacturability and reduce production costs.

b. HVAC Systems
We provide comprehensive HVAC solutions, including design, installation, and maintenance for commercial, industrial, and residential projects, ensuring comfort and energy efficiency.

1. System Design: Custom HVAC designs tailored to meet the specific needs of the facility.

2. Energy Efficiency: We focus on optimizing systems for maximum energy savings and sustainability.

3. Maintenance Services: Reliable maintenance and repair services to ensure optimal system performance.

c. Industrial Machinery and Automation
Our expertise in industrial machinery covers the full spectrum from design to commissioning, ensuring high performance and reliability for your manufacturing processes.

1. Custom Machinery Design: We create bespoke machinery solutions tailored to specific production needs.

2. Automation Solutions: Implementing automation systems to enhance productivity and reduce operational costs.

3. Installation and Commissioning: Comprehensive services to ensure smooth installation and start-up of machinery.

d. Piping and Plumbing Services
TST offers expert piping and plumbing solutions for various industries, ensuring leak-free and efficient systems that meet all regulatory standards.

1. Piping Design: We design robust piping systems for efficient fluid transport.

2. Plumbing Installation: High-quality plumbing services for both commercial and industrial projects.

3. System Maintenance: Regular maintenance services to ensure optimal performance of piping systems.

e. Energy Solutions
We are committed to delivering sustainable energy solutions, helping clients reduce their carbon footprint and enhance energy efficiency.

1. Renewable Energy Integration: We design systems that incorporate renewable energy sources like solar and wind.

2. Energy Audits: Comprehensive audits to identify energy-saving opportunities and optimize consumption.

3. Energy Management Solutions: Customized strategies for effective energy use and cost savings.
